What's Analog Layout?

Analog layout involves designing the physical layout of analog integrated circuits, placing and routing transistors, capacitors, and other components to create functional circuits that meet performance specifications.

Unlike digital layout, analog requires careful consideration of parasitic effects, matching, and noise to ensure circuit performance.
